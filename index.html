About Elisa Ossino

Elisa Ossino is an architect and interior designer with a talented eye for geometry, space, and light. She also uses monochromatic colour palettes, letting the beauty of her shape and colour choices resonate with simplicity. Trained in architecture at the Politecnico di Milano, she currently runs her own studio, Elisa Ossino Studio, and teaches in the “Interactive Media for Interior Design” program at the IUAV University of Venice (De Padova, n.d., para. 1-4). At the Salone del Mobile, Elisa Ossino Studio has both held its own exhibitions and styled booths for other companies for over 15 years (Elisa Ossino Studio, n.d., para. 2).

Ossino moves fluidly between different forms of design, from interiors to products to art direction. Her versatility comes from being able to specifically analyze the details that make a project stand out and building a visual identity from there (Donati de Conti, 2020, para. 7). She is inspired by both the past and present, learning from design history while also allowing her pieces to reflect current moments in culture. The resulting projects are simultaneously modern and timeless (Donati de Conti, 2020, para. 9).
